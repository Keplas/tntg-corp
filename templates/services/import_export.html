THE PROCESS

How Import & Export Works

{% for step in steps %}{% endfor %}
1
Source & Verify

Identify and verify quality products or commodities from certified suppliers in the origin country, ensuring compliance with export standards.

2
Documentation

Prepare all required trade documents — commercial invoices, certificates of origin, packing lists, and customs declarations for smooth border clearance.

3
Customs Clearance

Handle customs procedures, duties and taxes at point of entry/exit. T&TG manages compliance with Canadian, Ugandan and Kenyan trade regulations.

4
Logistics & Shipping

Coordinate freight — air or sea — with trusted logistics partners. Full cargo tracking from origin warehouse to final destination.

5
Payment & Settlement

Secure multi-currency settlement across CAD, UGX and KES. Transparent foreign exchange rates with no hidden fees for our trade partners.

6
Delivery & After-Care

Final-mile delivery to buyers with post-shipment support. Our team follows up to ensure every consignment meets agreed quality and quantity standards.
