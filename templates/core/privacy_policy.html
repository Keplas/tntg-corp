Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2026  |  Effective immediately

T&TG Trade Corporation ("T&TG", "we", "us", or "our"), incorporated in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA, S.C. 2000, c. 5) and applicable Ontario privacy laws.

1. Who We Are

T&TG Trade Corporation is a registered Import & Export (RM) e-commerce company headquartered at 9 Summerbridge Rd, Toronto, ON M1G 1L8, Canada. We operate tntgcorp.com, an online shopping platform for premium green coffee products traded between Canada, Uganda, Kenya and USA (Ohio).

Privacy Officer Contact: tom.grouptrade@gmail.com  |  +1 (416) 832 3512

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: Full name, username, email address, phone number, country, city and password (stored encrypted)
  • Order information: Delivery address, destination country, product quantities, payment references
  • Financial information: We do not store credit card or mobile money details — payments are processed by third-party processors (Flutterwave/Stripe)
  • Loyalty programme data: Points earned, referral history, withdrawal requests
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, session cookies, page visit timestamps

3. Why We Collect It (Legal Basis — PIPEDA Principle 4)

  • Contract performance: To process orders, deliver products and manage your account
  • Legal obligation: To comply with Canadian import/export regulations, CFIA requirements and tax laws
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our platform, prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Consent: For marketing emails — you may withdraw consent at any time

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Manage your T&TG Loyalty Points account
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, withdrawal notifications)
  • Comply with Canadian Customs, CFIA food safety and import/export documentation requirements
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Respond to your customer service enquiries

5. Cookies (PIPEDA + Canadian Anti-Spam Legislation)

We use essential session cookies required for the platform to function (login sessions, CSRF protection). We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, you will be unable to log in or place orders.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Payment processors (Flutterwave, Stripe) — for payment authorisation only
  • Cloudinary — for image storage (profile photos, product images)
  • Render.com — our hosting provider (servers located in USA)
  • Canadian government authorities — when legally required (CBSA, CFIA, CRA)

Any international data transfer complies with PIPEDA Schedule 1, Principle 7 (safeguards).

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, plus 7 years after account closure as required by Canadian tax law (CRA record-keeping requirements under the Income Tax Act). Order records are retained for 7 years for audit purposes.

8. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by T&TG (within 30 days of written request)
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at www.priv.gc.ca

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Officer at tom.grouptrade@gmail.com.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including encrypted passwords (bcrypt), HTTPS/TLS encryption, secure session cookies, account lockout after failed login attempts, and withdrawal PIN verification for financial transactions.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not directed at persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified by email to registered users. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
