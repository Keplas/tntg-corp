About T&TG

Tom & The Group Trade Corporation

Founded on October 14, 2026, T&TG Trade Corporation operates a Trade & e-Commerce platform designed to facilitate seamless domestic and international commerce.

Our Toronto-based corporation operates globally, providing services across Canada, Uganda and Kenya. From our headquarters in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, we coordinate international operations that support cross-border collaboration, market development and service delivery.

T&TG Trade Corp is a registered Import & Export (RM) corporation, bringing quality textiles, clothing, shoes, watches and coffee to buyers across our three operating countries.
